Mission

Our mission is to decrease the number of missed diagnoses of hyperthyroidism through machine learning

Vision

Our tool uses EHR data-based models to output personalized risk scores for Hypothyroidism so that primary care physicians may order more lab tests to diagnose at-risk patients. All calculated risks are based on a limited list of factors; ThyroAid acts only as a precision medicine supplement in the clinic. For clinicians and pharmaceutical companies, ThyroAid will bring more patients and revenue to you, while improving public health.
