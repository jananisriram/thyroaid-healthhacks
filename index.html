Welcome to ThyroAid

What's the problem?

Three million cases of Hypothyroidism are
diagnosed each year in the US, while 13-23
million cases in the US exist undiagnosed.

Why ThyroAid?

This clinician tool helps doctors identify which
patients to investigate further, by highlighting
patient-specific factors that are shown
by ML to correlate significantly to Hypothyroidism,
yet are commonly overlooked. By highlighting
factors based on personalized data, we hope
to reduce underdiagnoses while saving
time and effort, thus diagnosing those
false negatives, and improving patient lives.

How does ThyroAid work?

Input Patient Data

Doctors can choose to input patient data either by uploading a pdf of the patient's electronic health record (EHR) or inputting the needed information manually.

->

Calculate Diagnosis

Using our decision tree machine learning algorithm, ThryoAid will consider all factors inputted to determine the most likely diagnosis and the percentage accuracy based on both numerical and categorical data.

->

Physician Follow-Up or Decision

With ThyroAid's recommended diagnosis, the physician can either choose to order additional lab tests to gather more data to make a diagnosis or he/she will be able to make a final decision on the diagnosis for the particular patient.
